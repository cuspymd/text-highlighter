What is Text Highlighter?

Have you ever wished you could mark important passages while reading web pages, just like you would with a physical highlighter pen? Text Highlighter is a Chrome extension that brings this familiar study habit to your digital reading experience, allowing you to highlight and save important text across any webpage.

Key Features

The core functionality is beautifully simple yet powerful. Select any text on a webpage, and with either a keyboard shortcut or the context menu, you can highlight it instantly. These highlights persist across browsing sessions, ensuring your important marks stay exactly where you left them.

Customizable Colors

Express yourself with a variety of highlighting colors. Whether you prefer classic yellow for general notes, green for important points, blue for definitions, or pink for questions, Text Highlighter lets you create your own color-coding system. Each color can represent different levels of importance or categories of information.

Highlight Management

All your highlights are automatically saved and can be managed through the extension's dashboard. You can easily review, organize, or remove highlights as needed. The extension also allows you to manage highlights on a per-site basis, giving you complete control over your digital annotations.

Perfect For

  • Students reviewing online study materials
  • Researchers collecting information from multiple sources
  • Professionals reviewing technical documentation
  • Writers and editors during the revision process
  • Anyone who wants to remember important information from web pages
